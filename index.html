    Scrum Artifacts

    • Product Backlog
    • Sprint Backlog
    • Increment

    Product Backlog

    An ordered list of everything that is needed in the product.

    Sprint Backlog

    Set of Product Backlog items selected for the Sprint, plus a plan for delivering the product Increment.

    Increment

    The sum of all the Product Backlog items completed during a Sprint and all previous Sprints.
